Unofficial Tangrams bookmarklet

Inspired by Basic Thinking I wrote a small bookmarklet for you to easier search for a city. Drag this Tangrams bookmarklet to your bookmark bar, then head over to Vector Map and hit the bookmarklet. The URL will be searched for '&city=' and adapt the longitude and latitude for you :) 
https://tangrams.github.io/tangram-docs-assets/?procedural/tronish.yaml&city=Berlin#16/52.5170/13.3889
as an example where to manipulate the URL.
